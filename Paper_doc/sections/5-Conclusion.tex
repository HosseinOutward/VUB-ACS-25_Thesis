\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}
In this paper, we proposed a novel approach for distributed compression of gradients in federated learning, leveraging learned layered quantization techniques. We introduced two frameworks, the Simple Framework and the Intervaled Framework, to effectively compress gradients while maintaining model performance. Our experimental results demonstrated that our proposed methods significantly reduce communication overhead without compromising the accuracy of the global model. Future work will explore the integration of this quantization method with other vector compression techniques, investigating the effects of DSC quantization on rates of other compression methods.
