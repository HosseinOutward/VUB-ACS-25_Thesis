\section{Objectives and Contributions}
The primary objective of this thesis is to develop and validation of a learned Wyner–Ziv quantizer in FL settings. This aim is achieved by first developing a modular simulations suite alongside reproducibility notes. The performance of said quantizer will then be evaluated by comparing it to other baselines to get a better picture of where the improvement is coming from, aiding future researcher in finding optimal configurations for the task of learned DSC quantization.
