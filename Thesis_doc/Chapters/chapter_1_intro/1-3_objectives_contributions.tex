\section{Objectives and Contributions}
The primary objective of this thesis is to develop and validate a learned Wyner–Ziv quantizer in federated learning (FL) settings. This aim is achieved by first developing a modular simulation suite alongside reproducibility guidelines. The performance of the proposed quantizer will then be evaluated by comparing it to other baseline methods to better understand the sources of improvement, thereby aiding future researchers in finding optimal configurations for learned DSC quantization tasks.
