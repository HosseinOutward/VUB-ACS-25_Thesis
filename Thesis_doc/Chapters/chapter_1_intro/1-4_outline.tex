\section{Outline of the Thesis}

The remainder of this thesis is organized as follows: Chapter 2 provides a comprehensive review of the relevant literature, covering the basics of Information theory, DSC and distributed/federated learning, and also relevant works done by other researchers.

chapter 3 will be covering the methodology chooses including the training of a layered WZ quantizer, pre- / post-processing of the accumulated grads and the outline the main compression protocols used.

chapter 4 will present the quantitative results from each of the protocols in a structured manner and provides an in-depth discussion of these results, interpreting their significance

Finally, Chapter 5 concludes the thesis by summarizing the key findings and offering directions for future research
